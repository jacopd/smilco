How it works

A fixed, predictable path — you'll know the cost and the deliverable at every step.

  1. Readiness assessment

    A flat-fee review of your operation: where the hours go, where AI genuinely saves them, and what to ignore. You receive a written, prioritized plan — useful whether or not we build anything.

  2. Build & install

    We implement the highest-value items from the plan as fixed-scope projects, working with the tools you already use. No rip-and-replace.

  3. Support & keep current

    AI tools change monthly. We keep yours tuned, your team trained, and your policies current as the technology and the law move.
