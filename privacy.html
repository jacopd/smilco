Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 18, 2026

The short version

This website collects as little as possible. There are no accounts, no contact forms, no analytics trackers, and no advertising cookies. If you email us, we receive what you send — that's essentially it.

Information we collect

Information you send us. If you contact us by email, we receive your email address and the contents of your message. We use that information to respond to you and, if you become a client, to provide our services. We do not sell it, rent it, or share it with third parties for their marketing purposes.

Server logs. This site is hosted by GitHub Pages (GitHub, Inc.). Like most web hosts, GitHub may collect standard technical information from visitors — such as IP address, browser type, and pages requested — in server logs used for security and operations. That collection is described in GitHub's Privacy Statement. We do not receive or maintain those logs.

Fonts. This site loads typefaces from Google Fonts, which means your browser requests font files from Google's servers. Google's handling of those requests is described in Google Fonts' privacy notes.

What we don't do

We do not use analytics or advertising trackers; we do not set cookies; we do not buy, sell, or trade personal information; and we do not use the contents of your inquiries for any purpose other than responding to you and serving you as a client.

California residents

California residents may have rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act, including the right to know what personal information a business has collected and to request its deletion. Given the limited collection described above, exercising those rights is simple: email us at contact@smilco.com and we will tell you what we hold (typically, only correspondence you sent us) and delete it on request, except where retention is required for legal or professional obligations.

Retention

We retain correspondence for as long as reasonably necessary to respond to inquiries, maintain client relationships, and satisfy legal and professional obligations, after which it is deleted.

Children

This site is directed to businesses and is not intended for children under 16. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes

If our practices change — for example, if we add a contact form or analytics — this policy will be updated before those changes take effect, with a new effective date above.

Contact

Questions about this policy: contact@smilco.com.
